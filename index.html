Safety Tips

Here are some road safety tips that everyone should know to ensure safety while driving:

1. Stay focused: It is essential to focus on driving when you are behind the steering wheel. Refrain from using your smartphone, tablet or any other electronic device.


2. Do not drive in a bad mood: Negative emotions can adversely affect your concentration on the road.


3. Stop on Red: Always stop at red lights.


4. Obey traffic signals and signs: Follow posted speed limits and other traffic rules.


5. distractions: such as phone calls while driving.


6. wear a seat belt: This can help protect you in case of an accident.


7. These are just a few tips to help you stay safe on the road. Remember to always be aware of your surroundings and drive defensively.


Traffic Laws

Traffic signal laws vary by country and region. In general, traffic signals are designed to enable smooth traffic flow at intersections, crossroads and zebra crossings. Following traffic signal rules ensures that there are minimal road mishaps.


In India, for example, disobeying any mandatory traffic sign is a crime punishable by law2. The traffic control signals are based on a three-color light system: red, yellow, and green. The red light means stop, while the yellow means to wait or go slow, and the green light means that vehicles are allowed to go ahead.


In the United States, drivers should always use their turn signals when making turns or changing lanes. On freeways and highways, slower traffic generally stays in the right-hand lanes while faster drivers stick to the left-hand lanes.
